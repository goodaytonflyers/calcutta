Before the auction — Pool Setup tab

  • Set the buy-in amount, players per team, and number of bidding groups.
  • All payouts and max bids across the entire dashboard update instantly.
  • If team sizes are mixed, use 3 as the average or update mid-auction.

During bidding — Bid Calculator tab

  • When a team comes up, set their realistic ceiling round in the dropdown.
  • Type in the current bid as it climbs at auction.
  • Green = you're well within budget. Yellow = approaching your ceiling. Red = over your max — put your hand down.
  • The table below shows your profit or loss at every possible exit round.

The 70% rule (your brother-in-law's strategy)

  • Estimate the round a team will realistically reach.
  • Look up the cumulative payout for that round.
  • Multiply by 0.70 — that's your hard max bid.
  • Example: Sweet 16 ceiling at $192 payout → max bid = $134.

The loser pool fade

  • Two teams finish last and each collect 1% of the pool.
  • If you think a team will lose in R1, buy them cheap and root against them.
  • Use the Fade Calculator to check if the expected value is positive.

Logging purchases — Auction Tracker tab

  • Log every team bought during the auction to track total spend.
  • The burn rate bar turns orange at 60% spend, red at 85%.
  • Use this to pace yourself — don't blow your budget on early teams.
